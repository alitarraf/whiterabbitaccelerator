What We Learned
BUILT ON
THE BEST.

We studied YC, Techstars, and 500 Global — then adapted what works for small-town talent and community-first entrepreneurship.

Y Combinator

Talk to Users. Build. Iterate.

YC's core: ruthless focus on weekly milestones and evidence-based progress. Their office hours model drives accountability through consistent touchpoints.

  • Weekly milestones that produce undeniable proof of progress
  • Deep customer discovery before building anything
  • Demo Day as a high-stakes public accountability moment
  • Peer cohort creates organic pressure and knowledge sharing
Techstars

Mentorship at the Center.

Techstars' "Give First" philosophy puts mentors front and center. 74% of their companies raise capital within 3 years — because real relationships open real doors.

  • Surround founders with diverse expertise from day one
  • Community-driven model that works beyond Silicon Valley
  • Structured sessions prevent founder isolation
  • 74% of companies raise capital within 3 years
500 Global

Growth Focus + Diverse Founders.

500 Global proved world-class support works everywhere. Their 4-month format allows deeper iteration — and their diverse cohorts produce more resilient businesses.

  • 4 months allows deeper iteration than 3-month programs
  • International reach: great ideas exist outside tech hubs
  • Growth sprints generate traction before Demo Day
  • Diverse founder cohorts produce more resilient businesses
White Rabbit Adaptation
Our Edge

The Small Town Advantage.

We combine the best of all three — with one key difference: our community IS our network. Local roots mean local impact and real accountability.

  • Weekly 1:1 mentorship — not just open office hours
  • Real resources: Talent Maker City, SOREDI, White Rabbit Co-working
  • Community stakeholders attend Demo Day — not just investors
  • Job creation and economic development are core success metrics
